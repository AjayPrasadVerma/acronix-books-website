## Is Acronix Books really free?

Yes. Acronix Books is free during early access (v0.1.0). There's no per-invoice charge and no feature paywall on the accounting, GST and inventory features.

## Which platforms are supported?

Windows 10 and 11 (64-bit) and Linux (AppImage and .deb) today. The macOS build is in the pipeline — leave your email on the [download page](/download/) to be notified at launch.

## Does it work offline?

Completely. Acronix Books is **offline-first** — every feature works with no internet connection. [Cloud sync](/docs/cloud-sync/) is optional and only used when you choose to connect.

## Can it handle GST returns?

Yes. A dedicated GST module gives you the Sales, Purchase, Output Tax and Input Tax registers, a rate-wise HSN summary, and **GSTR-1** and **GSTR-3B** exported to Excel for the GST portal / offline utility. It also prepares **e-invoice** and **e-way bill** data from the same vouchers. See the [GST filing walkthrough](/docs/gst-filing/).

## How is my data protected?

Your entire book is encrypted at rest with **AES-256** (SQLCipher), unlocked by a password-derived key with a recovery code as backup. There's an idle auto-lock, a tamper-evident audit log and role-based access control. Full detail in [Security](/docs/security/).

## Can more than one person use the same book?

Yes. The envelope key vault supports **per-user key slots**, and capability-based **RBAC** with five role levels — Viewer, Operator, Accountant, Admin and Owner — lets you give each user only the access their role needs.

## Can I move from Tally to Acronix Books?

Acronix Books uses the familiar Indian accounting model — ledgers, groups, vouchers and voucher series — so the concepts carry over directly. It's designed to feel faster and more modern than legacy tools while staying GST-ready. See our blog post on choosing a [Tally alternative for GST billing](/blog/best-tally-alternative-gst-billing-2026/).

## How big a business can it handle?

The app virtualizes lists and stays responsive past **50,000 vouchers per financial year**, with sub-200ms interactions — comfortable for growing businesses.

## What happens to my data when the app updates?

Nothing — updates replace the application, not your book. Your encrypted database stays in place. See [Updating the app](/docs/updating-the-app/).

## How do I back up my data?

Create encrypted local backups any time, and optionally enable cloud sync for continuous, isolated encrypted backup with fresh-device restore. See [Backup & restore](/docs/backup-and-restore/) and [Cloud sync](/docs/cloud-sync/).

## Where do I get help?

Start with [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting/). Still stuck? Email support@acronixbooks.com with your app and OS version and the steps to reproduce the issue.
