Acronix Books is a modern **ERP and accounting desktop app** for Indian businesses of every size. It runs entirely on your own computer, works offline, and is built to be fast and keyboard-driven — while staying GST-ready out of the box.

This guide gives you the lay of the land. If you just want to get going, jump straight to [Installation](/docs/installation/).

## What you get

- **Accounting** — multi-company books, ledgers, account groups and ten voucher types (sale, purchase, receipt, payment, contra, journal, credit note, debit note, delivery challan and stock journal) through one shared posting engine.
- **GST** — tax categories and HSN codes on every item, the tax registers and HSN summary, **GSTR-1** and **GSTR-3B** with Excel export, plus **e-invoice** and **e-way bill** generation.
- **Inventory** — items, item groups, units, tax categories, warehouses, weighted-average / FIFO valuation, delivery challans, stock journals and job-work rates.
- **Reports** — day book, trial balance, P&L and balance sheet (both drillable), cash flow, outstandings and stock reports, all exportable to PDF and Excel.
- **Security** — your entire book is encrypted at rest with SQLCipher AES-256, protected by encrypted login, RBAC roles, idle auto-lock and a tamper-evident audit trail.
- **Cloud sync (optional)** — back up your whole book to your own isolated, encrypted cloud space and restore it on a new device.

## How it's different

<Callout type="tip" title="Offline-first, by design">
Acronix Books stores your data locally and works with zero internet connection. Cloud sync is entirely optional and something you turn on when you want it — your books are never held hostage by a network outage or a lapsed subscription.
</Callout>

Because everything runs locally, interactions are near-instant. Lists are virtualized, so a company with 50,000+ vouchers in a financial year stays responsive. And almost every action has a keyboard shortcut, so data entry is fast once the workflow is in your fingers.

## The fastest path to your first invoice

1. [Download and install](/docs/installation/) the Windows app.
2. [Create your first company](/docs/creating-your-first-company/) and set the financial year (April–March).
3. [Set up GST](/docs/tax-setup/) — your GSTIN, tax categories and HSN codes.
4. Create a sales voucher — see [Invoicing & vouchers](/docs/invoicing-and-vouchers/).
